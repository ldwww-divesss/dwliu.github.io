### About Me

I am an undergraduate researcher majoring in Intelligent Science and Technology at the College of Intelligent Robotics and Advanced Manufacturing, Fudan University. Maintaining a GPA of 3.87/4.0 (Rank: 2/27), I have established a rigorous foundation in computer science and artificial intelligence. I am actively preparing to pursue a direct Ph.D. program starting in the Fall of 2027.

My research vision centers on Large Language Model (LLM)-based Multi-Agent Systems (MAS). I am particularly dedicated to establishing scientific evaluation frameworks and exploring the mechanisms of emergence regulation under system scaling. To optimize organizational structures and agent heterogeneity, I integrate algorithms such as Reinforcement Learning (RL) and Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) into training frameworks. Beyond software-level MAS, I am deeply interested in embodied intelligence, investigating how cognitive architectures can be deployed in physical systems (e.g., bipedal robotics) to achieve robust, real-world interactions.
